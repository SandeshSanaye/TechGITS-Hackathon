Frequently Asked Questions

Q. Who can attend?

A. DMCE Hackathon is accepting all engineering college students and diploma students.

Q. When can I apply?

A. Applications will be accepted starting from 20th December till 20th January 2020.

Q. What Are The Registration Fees?

A. 300 Rupees per team of 3/4 individuals. Payment should be done via the mentioned payment gateways in Google form.

Q. For How Long Can We Work On The Project?

A. All participants can work for 30 hours on their projects. Participants are free to use open standard repositories, APIs and publicly available libraries.

Q. How Many Members Are Required In A Team?

A. No one likes to be a loner. You can come in a team size of 3-4 members.

Q. What If A Participant Is Hungry?

A. Eat-Code-Drink is the only objective of the Hackathon. And we'll take care of the first and last for you.

Q. Who Are The Judges For The Event?

A. Only the best for you. The panel of judges includes experienced professionals from various fields of technology and decisions made by organizers and judges are final.

Q. What Are The Prizes?

A. No one is a saint here, everyone here will want to win. And the awards are to satisfy your needs- there will be cash prizes, goodies, certificates, and a lot more awaits.
